Craig Banks Tailoring

Welcome to the newest addition to Edinburgh’s prestigious Thistle Street, specialising in the finest quality of bespoke configuration tailoring. 

Superb Quality

At Craig Banks the focus is on making highest-quality personally tailored attire that others will aspire to. Whether you are looking for business suits, formal wear, shirts or outerwear the attention to detail provided makes an event of dressing up, no matter the occasion. With a fantastic range of cloths for you to choose from, sourced from the world’s best mills, our eye for detail will guarantee you look and feel your best. 

Visit Our Store

British Design

Putting the emphasis on the best of British design and manufacturing in our accessories, we purvey a selection of goods from some of the UK’s best suppliers. Stocking hand-made cufflinks from Deakin & Francis, the best of Scottish cashmere from William Lockie and the most luxuriously finished night-wear from the world famous Derek Rose, the attention to quality extends into our own brand with all silk ties, scarves and handkerchiefs being hand made in Macclesfield. For leather goods and luggage the best in British design comes from Maxwell Scott Bags and is applied to the finest Italian leather that is hand made in Florence.

Tailored Experience

Offering the highest quality in made-to-measure clothing and accessories that rivals London’s finest, with a friendly and personalised shopping experience, makes Craig Banks the best option to meet your stylistic needs. To take advantage of the knowledge and expertise available in-store, please feel free to visit us at your earliest convenience.
