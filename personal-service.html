A Personal Experience

Having a shirt, suit or coat made can be a daunting task to the un-initiated, but with a little guidance and a lot of experience on our part, the process is relatively seamless. 

Beginning with a visit to the premises and a friendly chat, the type of garment, occasion for use and finish required will be ascertained. Next a refined selection of suitable cloth options will be presented for your selection whilst we detail how the overall look will come together. After only 7 weeks, a return visit to allow for minor adjustments may be required. Once accommodated, you take receipt of your unique, custom-tailored garment.
